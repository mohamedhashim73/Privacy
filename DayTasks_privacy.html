Important: DayTask is a completely offline application. All your task data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

DayTask does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. The app operates entirely offline and all data remains on your device.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Task Information: Task titles, descriptions, categories, priorities, dates, and completion status
  • App Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode), default category, default priority, and notification preferences
  • Statistics Data: Locally calculated statistics and analytics based on your tasks

This data is stored in a local database (SQLite) on your device and is never accessed by us or any third party.

2. Use of Data

All data stored by DayTask is used solely for the app's functionality:

  • To display and manage your tasks
  • To calculate and display statistics and analytics
  • To remember your app preferences and settings
  • To provide optional local notifications for task reminders (if enabled)

We do not have access to your data, and we do not use it for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

DayTask does not share any data with third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no data transmission, sharing, or selling of information.

4. Ads & Third-Party Services

DayTask does not display advertisements and does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app is completely free of ads and tracking.

5. Local Notifications

DayTask includes an optional local notifications feature that allows you to receive reminders for your tasks. This feature:

  • Is completely optional and can be enabled or disabled in the app settings
  • Operates entirely on your device using the device's local notification system
  • Does not require internet connection
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

6. Data Security

Your data security is maintained through:

  • Local storage on your device, protected by your device's security measures
  • No network transmission, eliminating risks associated with data breaches
  • No external server storage, ensuring your data cannot be accessed by unauthorized parties

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device. If you uninstall the app, all local data will be deleted.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time through the app's settings menu using the "Reset All Data" option. This will permanently delete all tasks and preferences stored locally on your device.

8. Children's Privacy

DayTask does not knowingly collect any information from children. Since the app does not collect any data externally and operates entirely offline, there are no privacy concerns regarding children's data.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
