Privacy Policy — Introduction

NOVA HR (“the App”) is a mobile workforce and human-resources companion for employees. It connects to a server address and account credentials that your employer (or the organization that issued your access) provides. This policy describes how information is handled when you use the App on your device.

The App is not an offline-only product: core features such as sign-in, attendance actions, requests, history, profile data, and in-app notifications depend on network access to your organization’s systems.

By using the App, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

Data Collection

Depending on how your organization configures the service and which features you use, the App may collect or process the following categories of information:

  • Account and session data: Server URL or base address, sign-in identifier (such as email), password or credentials you enter, and authentication tokens returned by your organization’s backend. Tokens and cached account-related data may be stored on the device using secure storage.
  • Workforce and HR data from your employer: Information retrieved over the network can include your profile, attendance records, time-off and approval requests, related documents or attachments, notification content, and reference data (for example, request types). This data originates from your organization’s systems, not from hidden profiling inside the App.
  • Attendance verification: Where enabled by your organization’s workflow, the App may capture images using the device camera and collect device location (including coordinates and a human-readable place description produced via platform geocoding) at check-in or check-out.
  • Documents you choose to attach: Files you select through the system file or photo picker for requests may be transmitted to your organization’s backend as part of those requests.
  • Device and app settings: Preferences such as language may be stored on the device. The App may perform on-device checks related to device integrity (for example, indications of jailbreak, root, or emulator use) to support organizational security expectations.
  • Optional biometric unlock: On supported devices, you may use Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint where the operating system provides it; biometric templates are managed by the platform, not by the App.
  • Technical logs (development): Diagnostic messages may be written to developer logs when debugging is enabled. These are not used for advertising and are not sold.

What the App does not do (based on the current application design): The App does not show third-party advertisements, does not use web browser cookies, and does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs for behavioral tracking in the shipped dependencies. Push notification code paths that rely on third-party push providers appear inactive in the current project configuration; work-related notices you see in the App are expected to come from data your organization exposes through its APIs when you use the App online.

Use of Data

Information processed through the App is used to:

  • Authenticate you and maintain a signed-in session with your organization’s backend.
  • Record and display attendance, time-off, approvals, and related history as authorized by your employer.
  • Transmit attendance evidence (such as photos and location) when your organization’s policies require it.
  • Show notifications and profile or HR information returned by your organization’s systems.
  • Store certain values locally (for example, session, language, or cached responses) so the App can function efficiently after sign-in.
  • Support optional on-device security checks requested by your organization’s risk policies.
  • Allow you to generate or share exports (such as PDFs) from data the App already displays, using platform printing or sharing where you initiate the action.

The purposes above are tied to employment or contractor workforce management. Your employer remains responsible for defining lawful bases, retention, and workplace rules that apply to you.

Data Sharing

Data you submit or that the App retrieves is shared with the systems your employer operates or designates (for example, a human-resources or enterprise backend). The exact legal entity, data processor, and integrations depend on your organization’s configuration.

The App operator does not sell your personal information. Data is not shared for behavioral advertising through this App.

Where the operating system provides maps or place-name resolution for coordinates, limited location-related queries may be processed by Apple, Google, or other platform services according to their respective policies; the App uses such services only to support location features you trigger.

If your organization uses additional infrastructure (such as hosting providers, identity systems, or messaging services) outside this App, those arrangements are controlled by your employer and their contracts.

Ads & Third-Party Services

The App does not display third-party advertising and does not use ad tracking frameworks in the dependency set described for this build.

Third-party components and platform services that may be involved when you use the App include: the device operating system; secure storage and biometric APIs; camera, photo library, and file-picker APIs; network stack and certificate authorities; geolocation and geocoding services provided by the platform; image loading from URLs your organization supplies; and libraries used for UI, networking, and PDF generation. Each is used to implement App functionality, not to run ads.

Data Security

The App is intended to communicate with your organization’s servers over encrypted connections (for example, HTTPS) when those servers are configured accordingly. Authentication tokens and sensitive cached values are stored using platform secure storage where implemented.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. You should protect your device with a strong lock screen, keep credentials confidential, and follow your employer’s security policies. If you believe your account is compromised, notify your employer and change credentials as directed.

Children’s Privacy

NOVA HR is intended for use by adults in a workforce context (employees or authorized personnel). It is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction for workforce apps). The App should not be used by children without appropriate employer authorization and supervision. If you believe a child’s information has been collected improperly through this App, contact us using the email below and your employer.
