Privacy Policy

Introduction

Masroof ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

Masroof is an offline-only application that operates entirely on your device. We collect and store the following information locally on your device:

  • Expense Data: Amount, category (Coffee, Transport, Food, Subscriptions, Other), optional notes, and timestamps for each expense you create
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information
  • No Account Data: Masroof does not require account creation or authentication

Data Storage

All expense data is stored locally on your device using the device's local storage system (SharedPreferences). This data:

  • Remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers
  • Is not backed up to cloud services by our application
  • Can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing app data
  • Is accessible only to you through the application

Use of Data

Your expense data is used solely within the Masroof application to:

  • Display your expense history and list
  • Calculate spending totals (today, this week, this month)
  • Generate category breakdowns and analytics
  • Provide smart insights about your spending patterns

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any other purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties, including:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics services
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Social media platforms
  • Any other external services or companies

Your data remains private and local to your device.

Ads & Third-Party Services

Masroof does not display advertisements and does not integrate with any third-party advertising or analytics services. The application operates independently without external service dependencies.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Data security depends on your device's security measures
  • We recommend using device lock screens, encryption, and other device security features
  • If you share your device with others, they may be able to access your expense data
  • Uninstalling the application will permanently delete all stored expense data

Children's Privacy

Masroof is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us immediately.
