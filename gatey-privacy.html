Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 23, 2026

Gatey ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our visitor entry and exit log application for apartment buildings.

Important: Gatey is an offline application. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Gatey collects the following information to provide visitor logging functionality:

  • Visitor Information: When you register a visitor, we collect:
    • Visitor name
    • Apartment number
    • Purpose of visit
    • Entry time (automatically recorded)
    • Exit time (when logged)
  • App Settings: Building name and notification preferences that you configure within the app

What We Do NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Payment information
  • Any data from third-party services

2. How We Use Your Information

All information collected by Gatey is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Creating and maintaining visitor entry and exit logs
  • Displaying visitor history and active visitor lists
  • Enabling search and filtering functionality within the app
  • Storing your app preferences and settings

We do not use your data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Analytics or tracking
  • Sharing with third parties
  • Any purpose outside of the app's core functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only: All data collected by Gatey is stored exclusively on your device using local storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences). No data is transmitted over the internet, stored on external servers, or synchronized to cloud services.

Data Security: While we implement standard security practices for local data storage, you are responsible for:

  • Securing your device with appropriate access controls (passwords, biometrics)
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using the app only on trusted devices

Data Deletion: You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the application from your device. All visitor logs and settings will be permanently removed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no mechanism for data sharing, cloud synchronization, or external data transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

Gatey does not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or data collection platforms. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

The app uses the following open-source libraries for core functionality:

  • Flutter framework (Google) - for app development
  • Provider package - for state management
  • SharedPreferences - for local data storage
  • Intl package - for date and time formatting

These libraries are used solely for app functionality and do not collect or transmit user data.

6. Children's Privacy

Gatey is designed for use by building security personnel and property managers. The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe that a child has provided information through the app, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • Access: You can view all visitor logs and data within the app
  • Modification: You can update visitor information or app settings at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual visitor records or all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Export: While the app does not provide built-in export functionality, data is stored in a standard format on your device

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
