Overview

“مخزون الملابس” is an inventory management app for clothing shops. It helps store owners and staff record and manage clothing items, monitor quantities, receive low-stock alerts, and view basic inventory reports (for example: totals by category and top colors).

The app is designed to operate without creating user accounts and without sending your inventory data to a developer-controlled server. In normal operation, data you enter is stored locally on your device using a local database (SQLite).

Key point: Your inventory data stays on your device. This version of the app does not include cloud sync or a backend that receives your inventory content.

Data Collection

We collect and store only the data you intentionally enter into the app for inventory management, including:

  • Clothing item details (e.g., name, category, size, color)
  • Inventory numbers (e.g., quantity, price/value)
  • Inventory movement log entries (e.g., add/remove quantities, timestamps, optional notes you type)
  • App preferences stored on-device (e.g., low-stock alert threshold)

We do not require or request you to provide:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or account credentials
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, call logs, SMS, photos, or files from your device
  • Biometric identifiers

Use of Data

Your on-device data is used only to provide app functionality, including:

  • Displaying and managing inventory items
  • Generating inventory summaries and reports
  • Showing low-stock alerts based on your configured threshold
  • Allowing search and filtering within your inventory

We do not sell your data and do not use it for advertising.

Data Sharing

We do not share your inventory data with third parties because the app does not upload your inventory data to a developer-controlled backend.

Your device or operating system may provide optional backup features (for example, iCloud or Google device backup) depending on your settings. Those backups are controlled by you and your platform provider under their policies.

Ads & Third-Party Services

This version of the app does not include advertising SDKs and does not include analytics or crash-reporting integrations intended to track you.

The app is distributed through platform app stores and runs using platform services (Android/iOS). Those platforms may collect information under their own policies when you download or use apps from their stores.

Data Security

Inventory data is stored locally on your device within the app’s private storage area using a local database. We do not operate a server that stores your inventory.

No method of storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing access to your device (e.g., screen lock) and managing backups. If you share your device, others may be able to access the app and the data inside it.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for business inventory management and is not designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.
