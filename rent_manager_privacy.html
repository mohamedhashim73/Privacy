Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

RentManager - Property Manager ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

RentManager is a fully offline application. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers or cloud services.

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Property Information: Property names, addresses, rent amounts, deposits, sizes, amenities, and status
  • Tenant Information: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, property assignments, and emergency contacts
  • Contract Details: Start dates, end dates, rent amounts, deposits, and payment schedules
  • Payment Records: Payment amounts, dates, status, methods, and monthly records
  • Expense Records: Expense categories, amounts, dates, descriptions, and property associations

We do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Personal information transmitted to external servers
  • Cookies or web tracking technologies

3. Use of Data

All data you enter into RentManager is stored exclusively on your device using local database storage (SQLite). This data is used solely to:

  • Manage your rental properties and tenant information
  • Track payments and expenses
  • Generate reports and export data (when you choose to do so)
  • Display local notifications for contract renewal reminders

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third-party analytics providers.

The App includes functionality to export data (such as CSV files and reports) that you can choose to share using your device's native sharing capabilities. This sharing is entirely under your control and occurs only when you explicitly initiate it.

5. Local Notifications

RentManager uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming contract renewals. These notifications are generated and stored locally on your device. We do not have access to your notification data, and notifications are not transmitted to any external service.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security measures
  • We recommend using device-level security features such as screen locks, biometric authentication, and encryption
  • If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted from your device
  • We recommend regularly backing up your device to preserve your data

7. Children's Privacy

RentManager is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since our App does not collect any data externally, this policy serves as a precautionary measure.

8. Third-Party Services

RentManager does not integrate with third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud storage providers. The App operates entirely offline and does not require internet connectivity to function.

The App uses the following open-source libraries for core functionality:

  • SQLite database for local data storage
  • Local notification services for reminders
  • File sharing capabilities (only when you choose to export data)

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • You can view, edit, or delete any data at any time within the App
  • You can export your data using the App's export features
  • You can delete all data by uninstalling the App
  • You can back up your data using your device's backup features

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
