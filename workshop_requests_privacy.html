Overview

طلبات الورشة helps spare-parts counters and workshops record missing parts requests and track their status (Pending, Ordered, Arrived). The app is designed to be fast and simple for day-to-day use.

Offline-first: The app works without an internet connection. Requests are stored locally on your device. The app does not provide account login or cloud sync.

Privacy Policy

Data Collection

The app collects and processes only the information you choose to enter in the app, such as:

  • Car model
  • Part name
  • Urgency level (Normal / Urgent / Critical)
  • Requester type (Customer or Workshop)
  • Requester name
  • Optional requester phone number
  • Optional notes
  • Request status and timestamps (created/updated/ordered/arrived)

The app does not collect:

  • Account identifiers (no sign-in is available)
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts, photos, microphone, camera data
  • Browsing data, cookies, or web tracking
  • Analytics or advertising identifiers

Use of Data

Your locally stored data is used to provide in-app functionality, including:

  • Displaying and searching requests
  • Filtering by status and requester type
  • Generating simple reports (status counts, most requested parts)
  • Showing local notifications when a request is marked as “Arrived” (optional)

Local Storage

Requests are stored on-device using a local database (SQLite). App settings (such as theme and notification toggle) are stored on-device (SharedPreferences).

Data Sharing

The app does not send your request data to the developer or to external servers. The app does not sell or share your data with third parties.

Ads & Third-Party Services

The app does not include ads or in-app analytics/tracking. The app may use platform notification capabilities to display local notifications on your device.

Permissions

If you enable notifications and your device requires permission, the app may request notification permission (for example, Android POST_NOTIFICATIONS). Denying permission will not prevent you from using the app; it only affects notification delivery.

Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. You are responsible for controlling access to your device. If your device is compromised, rooted/jailbroken, or shared without safeguards, your data may be exposed.

Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for business/workshop use and is not designed for children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
